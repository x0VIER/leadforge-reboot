# Research Standards

## Source priority

1. Official company website
2. Official team or about page
3. Public state corporate registry
4. Public business directory or map listing

## Clean-lead rules

- No guessed owners
- No private contact scraping
- No fake emails from asset filenames, icons, or scripts
- No merge into master until the row is reviewed
- Prefer owner, founder, president, CEO, or manager contacts when publicly visible

## Review checklist

1. Confirm the company is real and active from public evidence.
2. Confirm the website and phone are usable.
3. Confirm the owner or executive field is grounded in a source URL when available.
4. Confirm the visible gap and offer angle are specific, not generic filler.
5. Confirm the row will not duplicate an existing business/website in the master file.
